As sales cycles evolved, practitioners added letters to MEDDIC to capture two important factors: the customer's internal Paper Process for purchases, and the Competition in the deal.

  <div>

    <div className="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 mb-4">

    </div>
    <div className="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 mb-4">

    </div>
    <div className="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">

        <div className="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
          Quantifiable outcomes the prospect cares about.
        </div>
        <div className="mt-3 p-3 bg-purple-50 dark:bg-purple-900/20 rounded">
          <div className="text-sm italic">Example: "Increase production throughput by 20%" or "Reduce operational costs by $2M annually"</div>

      </div>
      
      <div className="bg-white dark:bg-gray-800 p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md hover:shadow-lg transition-all">
        <h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-blue-600 dark:text-blue-400 mb-3">E - Economic Buyer</h3>
        <div className="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
          The person with final approval authority.
        </div>
        <div className="mt-3 p-3 bg-blue-50 dark:bg-blue-900/20 rounded">
          <div className="text-sm italic">Example: The CFO who signs off on budgets or the CIO with final approval authority</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div className="bg-white dark:bg-gray-800 p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md hover:shadow-lg transition-all">
        <h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-green-600 dark:text-green-400 mb-3">D - Decision Criteria</h3>
        <div className="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
          How the decision will be judged.
        </div>
        <div className="mt-3 p-3 bg-green-50 dark:bg-green-900/20 rounded">
          <div className="text-sm italic">Example: Must integrate with existing systems, deliver ROI within 12 months, meet security standards</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div className="bg-white dark:bg-gray-800 p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md hover:shadow-lg transition-all">
        <h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-amber-600 dark:text-amber-400 mb-3">D - Decision Process</h3>
        <div className="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
          How the decision will be made, steps and timeline.
        </div>
        <div className="mt-3 p-3 bg-amber-50 dark:bg-amber-900/20 rounded">
          <div className="text-sm italic">Example: RFP this quarter, pilot next quarter, final board approval by year-end</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div className="bg-white dark:bg-gray-800 p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md hover:shadow-lg transition-all">
        <h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-red-600 dark:text-red-400 mb-3">P - Paper Process</h3>
        <div className="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
          The formal process to finalize the purchase – procurement, legal, paperwork, contract approvals, compliance checks, etc.
        </div>
        <div className="mt-3 p-3 bg-red-50 dark:bg-red-900/20 rounded">
          <div className="text-sm italic">Example: Security review, board approval for contracts over $500k, legal review process</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div className="bg-white dark:bg-gray-800 p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md hover:shadow-lg transition-all">
        <h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-indigo-600 dark:text-indigo-400 mb-3">I - Identify/Implicate Pain</h3>
        <div className="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
          The pain points driving the deal – often expanded as "Implicate the pain," meaning not only identify the pain but also tie it to larger business implications.
        </div>
        <div className="mt-3 p-3 bg-indigo-50 dark:bg-indigo-900/20 rounded">
          <div className="text-sm italic">Example: Frequent stock-outs costing $50K monthly, ERP system failures causing production delays</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div className="bg-white dark:bg-gray-800 p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md hover:shadow-lg transition-all">
        <h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-violet-600 dark:text-violet-400 mb-3">C - Champion</h3>
        <div className="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
          An internal advocate who actively supports your solution inside the customer's organization.
        </div>
        <div className="mt-3 p-3 bg-violet-50 dark:bg-violet-900/20 rounded">
          <div className="text-sm italic">Example: The VP of Operations who desperately wants a new solution and will lobby internally</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <div className="bg-white dark:bg-gray-800 p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md hover:shadow-lg transition-all">
        <h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-rose-600 dark:text-rose-400 mb-3">C - Competition</h3>
        <div className="text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300">
          Who and what you're competing against – not just direct competitors, but any alternative uses of budget or internal solutions.
        </div>
        <div className="mt-3 p-3 bg-rose-50 dark:bg-rose-900/20 rounded">
          <div className="text-sm italic">Example: Incumbent vendor pushing an upgrade, prospect considering building internally</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
