# AmolinoAI Privacy Policy

Your information is primarily used to provide and improve our services, respond to your inquiries, and send you updates or notifications. We do not sell, lease, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers.

## Information We Collect and How We Use It

AmolinoAI collects and processes user data to enhance sales opportunity insights and streamline sales processes. Specifically, if you grant access to your Google account, we access the following data:

- **Google Email Data:**  
  We analyze email content to detect sales opportunities, interactions with prospects, and engagement levels.
- **Google Calendar Data:**  
  We use calendar events to identify meetings with customers, track meeting frequency, and enrich sales opportunity data with relevant scheduling information.

This data helps us provide accurate sales intelligence, automate data entry, and improve opportunity tracking.

We do not use this data for advertising purposes or share it with third-party marketers. **We DO NOT transfer or disclose your information to third parties for any purpose.**

## Security of Your Information

### Emails, Calendar, and Personal Data

All emails and personal information collected by [https://amolino.ai](https://amolino.ai) are securely stored and protected against unauthorized access or use. Our security measures include:

- SSL encryption
- Regular security audits
- Employee privacy training
- Following best-in-class security coding practices

### Access Control

Access to sensitive data, including personally identifiable information, is strictly limited. Only individuals who need access to this data to perform their job duties are granted permission, ensuring the highest level of confidentiality and security.

## Use of Data for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

We are committed to upholding the highest standards of data privacy and ethical use of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) technologies. In alignment with this commitment:

### Non-Personalized Models

We do not use any user data obtained from providers like Google Workspace or Microsoft to train non-personalized AI and/or ML models. Our development and enhancement of AI and ML technologies are strictly confined to data that does not identify or relate to an individual user.

### Data Retention and Use

User data obtained through providers like Google Workspace or Microsoft, including but not limited to APIs, is not retained for the purpose of developing, improving, or training non-personalized AI and/or ML models. Our use of user data is strictly limited to creating, training, or enhancing machine learning or artificial intelligence models that are personalized and directly relevant to the user's specific interactions with our services.

### Data Transfer and Sale

We do not transfer, sell, lease, or in any way distribute any user data for the purpose of creating, training, or improving machine learning or artificial intelligence models that are non-personalized. Our practices ensure that user data is solely utilized for enhancing the user's experience and the personalized features they receive from our services. This approach ensures that your data is not only secure but also used in a manner that respects your privacy and the personalized nature of our services.

Should you have any questions or require further clarification regarding our use of data for AI and ML purposes, please do not hesitate to contact us at **hello(at)amolino(dot)ai**.
